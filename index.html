Get Started Walkthrough

I am a Student →
Step 1

Visit the Website

Open your browser " here " to start the application safely.

Step 2

Click Get Started

Find and click the "Get Started" button in the center of your screen.

Step 3

Select "I am a Parent"

Select the "I am a Parent" option on the account setup screen.

Step 4

Verify Safaricom Line

You can use a Safaricom line or a stable Wi-Fi connection to move to the next step.

Step 5

Choose a Payment Plan

  1. Review the available payment options.
  2. Choose the plan that works best for your family.
  3. Complete the checkout process.
  4. Wait for payment confirmation.
  5. Get automatically redirected to your dashboard.
Flow order: 5A → 5B → 5C

Step 6

Add Learners

Select the "Add New Learner" then fill in the registration form with the learner’s Name, Grade level, and County, and submit to generate the student index code.

Step 7

Manage Learners & Secret Login

Open "Manage Learners". Review student details. From there, you can either:

  • View student activity and performance details, or
  • Use the "Login" element link to open and audit daily child engagement logs directly.
